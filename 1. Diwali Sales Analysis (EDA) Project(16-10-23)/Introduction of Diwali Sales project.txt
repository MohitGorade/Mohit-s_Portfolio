In this project i conducted a comprehensive analysis of Diwali sales data for a prominent website, delving into consumer behavior and market trends. By scrutinizing product preferences, pricing dynamics, and the influence of online channels, the analysis unveiled valuable insights to guide strategic decisions. The project not only shed light on past performance but also provided predictive insights, empowering the business to optimize inventory, enhance marketing strategies, and navigate the dynamic landscape of Diwali sales with a data-driven approach.
